About the Rivers Project

The Rivers Project aims to provide accessible and up-to-date information on river levels and conditions, primarily focusing on the Pacific Northwest.

Our goal is to help enthusiasts, professionals, and the general public stay informed about water resources.

Beta Information

Please note: This project is currently in Beta. We are actively working on improving features, data accuracy, and overall user experience. Some functionalities may be incomplete or subject to change. Your feedback is highly appreciated during this phase.

Data Sources

River level data is primarily sourced from the U.S. Geological Survey (USGS) National Water Information System. We strive to present this data in a user-friendly format.
